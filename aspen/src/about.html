About
Aspen HR

Our mission is to provide great service and technology for HR and payroll services in a cost-effective manner. We want our clients to pay less for more.

History

Aspen began as a business unit of Standish Management, LLC, a leading Venture Capital and Private Equity Fund Administration provider and is now a separate, indepdently-owned entity. As trusted advisors to their clients. Standish was unsatisfied with the service and solutions provided by other HR, benefits, payroll and PEO vendors. Generally, clients were over paying for sub-par solutions, dissatisfied with the level of service provided, and/or the solution wasn't appropriate for their needs. Aspen was created to specifically solve these problems.

Goals

Our goal is to be your high touch HR, payroll, and benefits solutions that allows you to customize your solution. You only pay for the services you need.

Our highly knowledgeable team, backed by a turn-key cloud based HR platform, allows you to focus on what you do best and to leave the rest to us.

Our Solution

The Aspen solution fits best for small to medium sized privately held companies. Our client base and expertise lies within alternative investment management companies, professional services firms, technology and life sciences portfolio companies, and foundations/nonprofit organizations.

In addition to high touch HR, payroll and benefits administration, we provide benefit design services so that your company can access special insurance and retirement products tailored specifically for owners.

Our Team

Servicing your company and its employees is important, so having an experienced and dedicated team is imperative. Meet Aspen's team who strives everyday to exceed your expectations.

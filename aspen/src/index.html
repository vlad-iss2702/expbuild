WHAT WE DO

Payroll & Tax Processing

Stay focused on your business while quickly and easily managing payrolls. Your payroll data is automatically integrated with other Aspen Cloud functions, so you never have to rekey anything from one system to another. You can quickly review timesheets, process your payroll, and even see payroll status - on any device and at any time. In addition, your Company's payroll taxes are automatically filed. We take POA so we can remit tax pavements and correspond with federal and state employment agencies for you.

HR Consulting

Aspen is here to serve not only the employer, but employees and dependents too. By bringing best practices in human capital management to your organization, we will assist with everything from claims processing, ACA filings, FMLA, HIPAA, job postings, background checks, employee handbooks, HR posters, forms & templates, to performance reviews and termination.

Benefits Administration

Our team and affiliate partners can help procure, design, and administer group and voluntary benefits. There are no limits to what you can offer your employees – medical, dental, vision, group life/ AD&D, long term and short term disability. Additional benefits, like flexible spending plans, pre-tax commuter benefits, and retirement plans are all accessible solutions to help attract and retain key talent.

Cloud hr Software

Aspen's cloud software provides everything your organization need for high quality HR, while saving countless hours and reducing manual paper and excel tasks. Our software automates the HR experience, such as electronic onboarding, benefits enrollment, PTO tracking, and time tracking through the employee portal which is customized to your brand.

Retirement Plans

We help design and implement clientretirement plans that enable you to hire and retain the best employes.
